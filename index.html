Devil May Cry Series

Devil May Cry is a hack-n-shlash game where your primary objective is to clear waves of devils in order to complete a level. This game also includes different types of puzzles that you can enjoy or even make you stuck in a certain level. What make this game fun is its' combo mechanics. Mixing light and heavy attacks along with your analog stick to do a satisfying combo depending on your style.
