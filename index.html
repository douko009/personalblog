My Personal Favorite Games and Video Game Genre

By: Villaruel Iel

Disclaimers

The list included below are my personal favorite games that i have expirienced playing. The game listed are not in order Thank you for understanding.

HERE ARE MY PERSONAL LIST OF GAMES

Devil May Cry Series

Devil May Cry is a hack-n-shlash game where your primary objective is to clear waves of devils in order to complete a level. This game also includes different types of puzzles that you can enjoy or even make you stuck in a certain level. What make this game fun is its' combo mechanics. Mixing light and heavy attacks along with your analog stick to do a satisfying combo depending on your style. This series got five game releases which never fails to give you a very impressive gameplay, huge bosses and epic story line.

Fighting Games

Fighting games are everywhere, in the arcades, consoles or even in your smart phones. Not a lot of gamers appreciate this genre for some reasons, but for me, i'll buy and play every game i see in the store. There three fighting games that i've been playing a lot recently. Tekken, Street Fighter and Dead or Alive. Though they are different games with different deveolopers,they still share alot of fighting game mechanics. Mixing LOW, MIDDLE, HIGH Puches or Kicks to do a very satisfying "10 HIT!" combo to defeat your friends or other people online. Execute your SPECIAL MOVES to seal your overwelming victory!

If you like this type of Games i recommend: Tekken series, Street Fighter series, Dead or Alive series and Guilty Gear series.

Multi-player Online Battle Arena Games (MOBA)

MOBA is one of the most popular genre right now around the globe. MOBA is everywhere, in your computers, mobile phones and consoles. Because of this genre and other competivive players, E-sports was stablished and it is dominated with the most popular MOBA game titles such as DOTA 2, League of Legends and Overwatch. I started playing MOBA years ago and i found them pretty hard to master. There are a lot of creative Characters you can use in order to create a dream team setup for you to win a match. They have classes such as Carries, Tanks, Supports and Mages. Capture the main objectives and proper team work is the key victory in this genre. So if you are into MOBAs, i recommend you my three favorite MOBA games, League of Legends, Battlerite, Overwatch.

If you are into this type of Games, I recommend: League Of Legends, Dota 2, Overwatch, Onmyoji Arena (mobile), Heroes of the Storm and Smite.

Open World Games

One of the most classic yet most addicting genre of all time, Open World Games. Who doesn't like Grand Thief Auto series, Assassins Creed or Saints Row? All of those games are Open worlds with very good story line.
Open World Games are the games that has lively environment like we have, big cities to explore and things we can interact with. This genre usually got "missions" that needs time to complete. You have to find your own ways to complete all the objectives and do it with your own style. Watch Dogs is a good example of an open world game that you won't get bored playing since you can do almost anything to complete a mission by hacking different things by your own advantage. Most Open World games have huge cast of Characters that you can communicate or interact with for your mission or side missions. Just like in the movies, they have character developments as the story goes by.

If you like this type of game, i recommend you to play: GTA series, Far Cry series, Assassins Creed series, Fallout and Skyrim.
